<p><p>The Open Die Forging Press market comprises various types, including Push-down Forging Presses, Pull-down Type Forging Presses, and others. Push-down Forging Presses operate by applying force downward, allowing for the shaping of large metal pieces without confinement. Pull-down Type Forging Presses, on the other hand, exert force by pulling the die downwards, enhancing control over the forging process. Other market types may include specialized presses designed for unique applications, catering to diverse industries requiring specific forging capabilities.</p></p>
