<p><p>The steel seamless pipes market is categorized into hot-rolled and cold-rolled types. Hot-rolled steel seamless pipes are produced at elevated temperatures, resulting in greater ductility and toughness, making them suitable for high-stress applications. In contrast, cold-rolled steel seamless pipes are formed at room temperature, offering superior surface finishes and tighter tolerances, ideal for applications requiring precision. Both types serve diverse industries, including oil and gas, construction, and transportation, catering to differing performance and structural requirements.</p></p>
