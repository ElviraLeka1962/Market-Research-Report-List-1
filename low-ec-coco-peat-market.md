<p><p>Low EC Coco Peat is a type of growing medium derived from coconut husks, processed to have low electrical conductivity (EC), making it suitable for horticulture and agriculture. Its low EC ensures it is free from excess salts, making it ideal for sensitive crops and seedlings.</p><p>The Low EC Coco Peat Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for sustainable and organic farming practices.
