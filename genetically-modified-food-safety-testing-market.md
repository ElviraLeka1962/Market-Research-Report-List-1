<p><p>The genetically modified food safety testing market encompasses various methods, including immunoassay and polymerase chain reaction (PCR). Immunoassays utilize antibodies to detect specific proteins or antigens from genetically modified organisms (GMOs), offering a rapid and sensitive means of testing. PCR, on the other hand, amplifies DNA sequences to identify genetic modifications at the molecular level, providing precise and reliable results. Both methods are essential for ensuring compliance with food safety regulations and consumer protection regarding GMOs in the food supply.</p></p>
