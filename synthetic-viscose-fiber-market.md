<p><p>The synthetic viscose fiber market includes two main types: Viscose Filament Yarn and Viscose Staple Fiber. Viscose Filament Yarn is a continuous fiber used primarily in the textile industry for making fabrics, offering a smooth texture and sheen. In contrast, Viscose Staple Fiber consists of shorter fibers that are spun into yarn, making it suitable for a variety of applications, including clothing and home textiles. Both types provide softness, breathability, and versatility in garment production.</p></p>
