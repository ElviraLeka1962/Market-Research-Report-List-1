<p><p>Photolithography chemicals for the semiconductor market encompass several key types essential for microfabrication. Silicon wafers serve as the substrate for circuit fabrication. Photoresists, light-sensitive materials, define patterns on wafers through exposure to UV light. Hexamethyldisilazane (HMDS) is a common adhesion promoter for improved photoresist application. Photoresist ancillaries include developers and strippers that process the photoresist film. Additional chemicals may include solvents and cleaning agents, collectively supporting the intricate manufacturing of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
