<p><p>The electric recreational vehicle (EV) market encompasses two main types: towable RVs and motorized RVs. Towable RVs, such as travel trailers and fifth wheels, are designed to be towed by a separate vehicle, offering flexibility and lighter weight. Motorized RVs, including Class A, B, and C vehicles, integrate a driving mechanism within the unit, allowing for direct travel without a tow vehicle. Both types leverage electric power to enhance sustainability, improve efficiency, and reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
