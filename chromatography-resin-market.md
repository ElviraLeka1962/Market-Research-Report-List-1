<p><p>The chromatography resin market encompasses various types used for separating and purifying biomolecules. Size exclusion chromatography resin separates molecules based on size, while ion exchange resins separate based on charge. Hydrophobic interaction resins utilize hydrophobic interactions for separation, and mixed-mode resins combine multiple mechanisms for versatility. Affinity chromatography resins specifically target and isolate proteins, with Protein A chromatography resins being specialized for antibody purification. Each type serves unique applications in bioprocessing and analytical chemistry.</p></p>
