<p><p>Infrared combustible gas sensors are vital in detecting flammable gases in various environments. The market comprises two main types: point detectors and open path detectors. Point detectors are localized instruments that sense gas concentration at a specific location, ideal for confined spaces. In contrast, open path detectors continuously monitor larger areas by analyzing the infrared light along a defined path to detect gas presence. Both types enhance safety measures across industrial, commercial, and residential applications.</p></p>
