<p><ul><li>Needle-Free Injectors</li><li>Pen Injectors</li><li>Wearable Injectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-injection devices market comprises three main types: needle-free injectors, pen injectors, and wearable injectors. Needle-free injectors utilize high-pressure technology to deliver medication through the skin without needles, enhancing comfort and reducing injection anxiety. Pen injectors offer a portable and user-friendly solution, allowing patients to easily administer precise doses of medication with adjustable settings. Wearable injectors provide continuous subcutaneous delivery of drugs over an extended period, improving patient adherence and convenience, particularly for chronic conditions requiring ongoing treatment.</p></p>
