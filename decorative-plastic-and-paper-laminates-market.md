<p><p>The decorative plastic and paper laminates market is divided into two main types: low-pressure and high-pressure laminates. Low-pressure decorative laminates are typically produced using heat and adhesive under lower pressures, resulting in cost-effective and versatile materials suitable for various applications. In contrast, high-pressure decorative laminates are manufactured under significantly greater pressures and temperatures, yielding highly durable and resistant surfaces, making them ideal for demanding environments. Both types serve diverse sectors, including furniture, flooring, and interior design.</p></p>
