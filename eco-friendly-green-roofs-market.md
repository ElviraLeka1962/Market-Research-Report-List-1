<p><p>The eco-friendly green roof market is categorized into three main types: extensive, semi-intensive, and intensive green roofs. Extensive green roofs are lightweight and primarily composed of drought-resistant plants, requiring minimal maintenance. Semi-intensive roofs offer a blend of features, supporting a wider variety of vegetation, including small shrubs, while needing moderate upkeep. Intensive green roofs are structured to support larger plants, including trees, necessitating deeper soil and significant maintenance but providing diverse benefits, such as enhanced biodiversity and improved insulation.</p></p>
