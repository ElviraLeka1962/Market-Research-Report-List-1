<p><strong>The PU Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PU sealants are categorized into two main types: single component and multi-component. Single component sealants are pre-formulated and can cure upon exposure to moisture, making them easy to use and ideal for various applications without the need for additional mixing. In contrast, multi-component sealants require the precise mixing of two or more components before application, allowing for customized properties such as enhanced durability or flexibility. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, and industrial sectors for sealing and bonding purposes.</p></p>
