<p><p>The electric spray gun market is primarily divided into two types: corded and cordless electric spray guns. Corded electric spray guns are powered by an electrical outlet, offering consistent performance and unlimited runtime, making them ideal for larger projects. In contrast, cordless electric spray guns run on rechargeable batteries, providing portability and ease of use in hard-to-reach areas, though they may have limited runtime. Both types cater to various users, including DIY enthusiasts and professional painters, enhancing efficiency in painting tasks.</p></p>
