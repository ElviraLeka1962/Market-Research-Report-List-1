<p><p>The hydrogen fuel storage system market is categorized into four types based on material and design. **Type I** uses all-metal construction, typically steel, suitable for low-pressure storage. **Type II** combines a metal liner with a composite overwrap, offering higher pressure capacity. **Type III** employs a non-metallic liner (e.g., polymer) with a full composite wrap, ideal for lightweight, high-pressure applications. **Type IV** features a polymer liner with a full composite wrap, optimized for lightweight, high-pressure storage, commonly used in fuel cell vehicles.</p></p>
