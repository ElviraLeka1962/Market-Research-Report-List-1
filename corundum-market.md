Ruby, the red variety of corundum, is highly valued in jewelry and laser technology for its rarity and hardness. Sapphire, available in various colors (excluding red), is prized for gemstones, industrial uses, and high-durability applications like watch crystals and optical components. Each type serves unique industries based on its properties.</p></p>
