<p><ul><li>Para Aramid –PPTA (1414)</li><li>Meta Aramid –MPIA (1313)</li><li>Other Aramid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Kevlar fiber market comprises three main types: Para Aramid (PPTA 1414), Meta Aramid (MPIA 1313), and Other Aramid fibers. Para Aramid (PPTA 1414) is known for its high strength, heat resistance, and durability, widely used in bulletproof vests and industrial applications. Meta Aramid (MPIA 1313) offers excellent thermal stability and flame resistance, ideal for protective clothing and electrical insulation. Other Aramid fibers include specialized variants with unique properties for niche applications, such as aerospace or automotive industries, catering to specific performance requirements.</p></p>
