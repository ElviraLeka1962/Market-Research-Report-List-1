<p><strong>The Electrosurgery Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Unit</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrosurgery Unit market is primarily categorized into monopole and bipolar units. Monopole Electrosurgical Units use a single active electrode to cut or coagulate tissue, with the return electrode placed elsewhere on the patient's body, making them versatile for various surgical procedures. In contrast, Bipolar Electrosurgical Units feature a pair of electrodes, allowing for precise tissue interaction at the site, reducing thermal spread and enhancing safety, particularly in delicate surgeries. Both types are essential for efficient surgical outcomes.</p></p>
