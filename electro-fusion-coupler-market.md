<p><p>An electro-fusion coupler is a device used in the polypropylene pipeline industry to join two sections of pipe seamlessly. This technology utilizes electrical resistance heating to melt the surfaces of the coupler and the pipes being joined, creating a strong bond that ensures a leak-proof connection. Electro-fusion couplers are widely used in various applications, including gas, water, and industrial piping systems, due to their reliability and efficiency.</p><p>The Electro-fusion Coupler Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period.
