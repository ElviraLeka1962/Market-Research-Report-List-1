<p><p>Angle rolls are essential equipment used for bending metal sections into various shapes. The market is primarily divided into mechanical and hydraulic types. Mechanical angle rolls utilize gears and levers for bending, offering high efficiency for moderate bending tasks. In contrast, hydraulic angle rolls employ hydraulic pressure for more precise and powerful bending, making them suitable for thicker materials and complex shapes. Both types serve different industrial needs, with mechanical models typically favored for lighter applications and hydraulic models for heavy-duty tasks.</p></p>
