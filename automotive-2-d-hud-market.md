<p><ul><li>Combiner</li><li>Windshield</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive 2-D HUD market comprises two primary types: combiner and windshield displays. Combiner HUDs project information onto a transparent screen positioned between the driver and the road, enhancing visibility without obstructing the view. Windshield HUDs project data directly onto the vehicle's windshield, integrating seamlessly with the glass and offering a more immersive experience. Both types aim to improve driver awareness and safety by delivering critical information, such as speed and navigation, while minimizing distractions.</p></p>
