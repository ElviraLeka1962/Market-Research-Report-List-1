<p><p>The IoT smart proximity sensors market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. Capacitive proximity sensors detect changes in capacitance, useful for sensing non-metallic objects. Inductive proximity sensors respond to metallic objects by generating an electromagnetic field, ideal for industrial applications. Photoelectric proximity sensors utilize light signals to detect objects and are versatile for various settings. Other types may include ultrasonic and magnetic sensors, catering to specific use cases in automation, safety, and smart environments, enhancing connectivity and efficiency.</p></p>
