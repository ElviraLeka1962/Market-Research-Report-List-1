<p><p>Overbased calcium sulfonate grease is categorized primarily by its Total Base Number (TBN), with TBN300 and TBN400 being the most prominent types. TBN300 grease typically offers moderate alkalinity, suitable for general lubrication in various industrial applications. In contrast, TBN400 grease provides higher alkalinity, enhancing its ability to neutralize acids and protect against corrosion, making it ideal for more demanding environments like heavy machinery and marine applications. The choice between TBN300 and TBN400 depends on specific lubrication and protection requirements.</p></p>
