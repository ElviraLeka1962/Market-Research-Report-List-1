<p><strong>The Modelling Clay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Dry Clay</li><li>Air-Dry Clay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modeling clay market can be categorized into two main types: non-dry clay and air-dry clay. Non-dry clay typically refers to oil-based or polymer clays that remain pliable and can be sculpted multiple times without hardening. In contrast, air-dry clay hardens when exposed to air, making it ideal for projects that require permanence. Each type caters to different user preferences, with non-dry clay favored for detailed, reusable creations and air-dry clay preferred for durable, finished artworks.</p></p>
