<p><strong>The Helical Gearbox Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Helical Gear Drive</li><li>Bevel Gear Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helical gearbox market consists primarily of two types: helical gear drives and bevel gear drives. Helical gear drives feature angled teeth that provide smoother and quieter operation, making them suitable for high-speed applications. They excel in transmitting power between parallel shafts. In contrast, bevel gear drives facilitate power transmission between intersecting shafts, often at right angles, and are ideal for applications requiring changes in direction. Both types offer enhanced efficiency and reliability, catering to various industrial and mechanical needs.</p></p>
