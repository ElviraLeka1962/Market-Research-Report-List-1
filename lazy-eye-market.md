<p><strong>The Lazy Eye Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strabismic</li><li>Refractive Amblyopia</li><li>Deprivation Amblyopia</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lazy eye market encompasses three primary types of amblyopia: strabismic, refractive, and deprivation. Strabismic amblyopia occurs when misalignment of the eyes disrupts visual development, leading to reliance on one eye. Refractive amblyopia results from significant differences in vision between the two eyes due to uncorrected refractive errors. Deprivation amblyopia develops from obstruction of visual input, such as cataracts. Each type requires distinct treatment approaches, influencing the overall market dynamics for diagnostic and therapeutic solutions in amblyopia management.</p></p>
