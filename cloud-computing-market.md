<p><strong>The Cloud Computing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)</li><li>Platform as a Service (PaaS)</li><li>Software as a Service (SaaS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud computing markets are categorized into three main types: Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS). IaaS provides virtualized computing resources over the internet, allowing businesses to manage their infrastructure without physical hardware. PaaS offers a platform for developers to build, test, and deploy applications without worrying about underlying infrastructure. SaaS delivers software applications over the internet on a subscription basis, enabling users to access services without installation or maintenance concerns.</p></p>
