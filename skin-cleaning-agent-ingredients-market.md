<p><strong>The Skin Cleaning Agent Ingredients Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anionic Cleaner</li><li>Non-Ionic Cleaner</li><li>Amphoteric Cleaner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin cleaning agent market comprises three main types: anionic, non-ionic, and amphoteric cleaners. Anionic cleaners, characterized by negatively charged surfactants, effectively remove dirt and oils, making them popular for products like soaps. Non-ionic cleaners, with no electrical charge, provide a milder option that is less irritating, often used in sensitive skin formulations. Amphoteric cleaners can act as either anionic or cationic depending on the pH, offering versatility in formulations, catering to various skin types and cleansing needs.</p></p>
