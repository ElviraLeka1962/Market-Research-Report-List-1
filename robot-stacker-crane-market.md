Monocolumn Stacker Cranes feature a single support column, allowing for compact designs suitable for smaller spaces. They are ideal for light to moderate loads. In contrast, Two-column Stacker Cranes utilize two vertical supports, providing enhanced stability and greater lifting capacity, making them suitable for heavier loads and larger warehouses. Both types enhance efficiency and automation in material handling processes.</p></p>
