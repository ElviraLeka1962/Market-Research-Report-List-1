<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Air Flow Meter market comprises two main types: Analog and Digital. Analog Air Flow Meters measure air intake using mechanical components, providing continuous voltage signals proportional to airflow. They are cost-effective and widely used in older vehicles. Digital Air Flow Meters, on the other hand, use electronic sensors to provide precise, real-time data in digital format, enhancing fuel efficiency and emissions control. They are increasingly adopted in modern vehicles due to their accuracy, reliability, and compatibility with advanced engine management systems.</p></p>
