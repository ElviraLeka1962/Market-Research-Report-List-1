<p><p>The blood sugar test strips market is primarily categorized into three types: glucose oxidase, glucose dehydrogenase, and others. Glucose oxidase strips utilize an enzyme that reacts with glucose to produce an electrical signal, commonly found in traditional meters. Glucose dehydrogenase strips use an alternative enzyme that is less sensitive to oxygen, offering potential advantages in accuracy. Other types may include various innovative technologies or hybrid methods that enhance performance or usability, catering to diverse consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
