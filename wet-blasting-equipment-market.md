<p><p>Wet blasting equipment encompasses two primary market types: slurry and vapor. Slurry wet blasting uses a mixture of water and abrasive media to effectively clean and prepare surfaces, minimizing dust and reducing material consumption. It’s ideal for heavy-duty applications. Conversely, vapor wet blasting employs a vaporized water mist combined with abrasives, offering a gentler approach suitable for delicate surfaces. Both methods enhance surface finishes while improving safety and environmental conditions, catering to diverse industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
