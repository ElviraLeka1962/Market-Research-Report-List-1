<p><p>The lithium-ion secondary battery separator market comprises primarily two types: bilayer and trilayer separators. Bilayer lithium-ion battery separators consist of two distinct layers, typically combining different materials to enhance ionic conductivity and thermal stability. In contrast, trilayer lithium-ion battery separators feature three layers, offering even better performance by integrating additional properties, such as improved mechanical strength and safety. Both types are critical for optimizing battery efficiency, enhancing energy density, and ensuring safety in various applications, including electric vehicles and portable electronics.</p></p>
