<p><p>The salt dryer market comprises several types, each characterized by distinct drying methods. Centrifugal dryers utilize high-speed rotation to remove moisture from salt through centrifugal force. Airflow dryers employ heated air to evaporate moisture, enhancing efficiency. Heating dryers use direct or indirect heat to accelerate the drying process, making them effective for large-scale operations. Additionally, other specialized dryers exist, catering to niche requirements within the market, ensuring a range of solutions for varied salt drying needs.</p></p>
