<p><p>The kerosene lighter market is categorized into two main types: clamshell and non-clamshell. These are often preferred for their user-friendly design. Non-clamshell lighters, on the other hand, have a simpler, open design that allows for quick access to the flame but may lack some protective features. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
