<p><p>The thread gages market comprises two primary types: thread ring gages and threaded plug gages. Thread ring gages are used to measure external threads, ensuring they meet specifications for size and pitch, while threaded plug gages assess internal threads. Both types are crucial for quality control in manufacturing processes, particularly in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and tooling. They help ensure proper fit and functionality of threaded components, enhancing product reliability and performance.</p></p>
