<p><p>In the automotive cameras and LiDAR market, adhesives are essential for securing sensors and ensuring optimal performance. Dual cure adhesives combine both thermal and ultraviolet curing processes, allowing for versatile application and rapid bonding under different conditions. These are often used in demanding environments. Single cure adhesives, on the other hand, rely on one curing method, typically UV or heat, and are straightforward to apply.
