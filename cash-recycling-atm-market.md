<p><p>The Cash Recycling ATM market is divided into two main types: Independent Cash Recycling Modules and Embedded Cash Recycling Modules. Independent Cash Recycling Modules operate as standalone machines that manage cash recycling without connecting to a traditional ATM system. In contrast, Embedded Cash Recycling Modules are integrated directly within conventional ATMs, allowing them to recycle cash while performing standard ATM functions. Both types enhance cash management efficiency, reduce operational costs, and improve user experience by automating cash transactions and reducing the need for manual cash handling.</p></p>
