<p><p>The Commercial and Industrial Humidifiers market includes several types: Adiabatic humidifiers use the natural evaporation of water to add moisture to the air, promoting energy efficiency. Direct injection steam humidifiers introduce steam directly into the air, ensuring rapid humidity increase in large spaces. Heated tank humidifiers heat water in a tank to produce steam, which is then released into the environment. Each type caters to specific humidity needs, energy consumption levels, and space requirements in commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
