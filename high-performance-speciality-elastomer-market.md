<p><p>The High Performance Specialty Elastomer market is primarily divided into two types: Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) and Thermosetting Elastomers (TSE). TPEs are versatile materials that combine the properties of rubber and plastic, allowing for easy processing and recycling, making them ideal for applications like automotive components and consumer goods. In contrast, TSEs provide superior heat resistance and mechanical strength, suitable for high-stress applications such as seals, gaskets, and industrial products. Both types cater to various industries requiring durable and flexible materials.</p></p>
