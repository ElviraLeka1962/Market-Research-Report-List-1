<p><p>The Plastic Flexible Packaging Printing Machine market is primarily segmented into two types: Ink Jet and Gravure Printing. Ink Jet printing utilizes digital technology to apply ink in specific patterns, allowing for high customization and quick changes in design. In contrast, Gravure printing involves an engraved cylinder that transfers ink onto the substrate, offering high-quality output and efficiency for large production runs. Both methods cater to different production needs, with Ink Jet being more suited for short runs and Gravure for mass production.</p></p>
