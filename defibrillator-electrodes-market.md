<p><strong>The Defibrillator Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implanted Defibrillator Electrodes</li><li>External Defibrillator Electrodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The defibrillator electrodes market is segmented into two main types: implanted defibrillator electrodes and external defibrillator electrodes. Implanted defibrillator electrodes are integrated into devices like pacemakers and ICDs to provide continuous cardiac monitoring and deliver shocks when necessary. In contrast, external defibrillator electrodes are used in emergency situations, typically during CPR, to deliver electric shocks from external devices to restore normal heart rhythm. Both types are crucial for effective cardiac emergency management and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
