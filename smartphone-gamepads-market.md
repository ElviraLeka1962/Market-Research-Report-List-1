<p><p>The smartphone gamepad market can be categorized into two main types: bracket-mounted and clip-mounted gamepads. Bracket-mounted gamepads securely attach to the smartphone, providing a fixed grip and enhancing stability during gameplay. They offer a more integrated experience, often resembling traditional game controllers. On the other hand, clip-mounted gamepads use a flexible clip system to hold the smartphone in place, allowing for easier portability and adaptability with various phone sizes. Both types cater to gamers seeking improved control and comfort.</p></p>
