<p><p>The thermoplastic solenoid valves market consists of three main types: flanged, jointed, and threaded. Flanged valves have flat surfaces with holes for bolts, offering strong connections, often used in larger systems. Jointed valves utilize mechanical joints for assembly, allowing for flexible installation in varied orientations. Threaded valves feature male or female threads for easy connection to pipes, ideal for smaller systems where quick assembly is essential. Each type caters to specific application needs in fluid control and management.</p></p>
