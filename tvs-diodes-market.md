<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TVS diodes market is categorized into two main types: uni-polar and bi-polar TVS diodes. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to protect circuits from positive transients, making them suitable for applications where only positive overvoltages are likely. In contrast, bi-polar TVS diodes can handle both positive and negative transients, offering a broader range of protection. This versatility makes bi-polar TVS diodes ideal for applications with fluctuating voltages, contributing to their growing popularity in various electronic devices.</p></p>
