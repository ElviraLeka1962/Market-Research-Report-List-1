<p><ul><li>Battery Powered Driver</li><li>Manual IO Needles</li><li>Impact Driven Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intraosseous infusion device market comprises three main types: battery-powered drivers, manual IO needles, and impact-driven devices. Battery-powered drivers provide controlled and consistent insertion, enhancing ease of use. Manual IO needles require physical force to penetrate the bone, offering low-cost and straightforward options but may demand more skill. Impact-driven devices utilize a rapid force delivery mechanism for quick access to the bone marrow, ensuring efficient fluid administration in emergency situations. Each type serves crucial roles in critical care environments.</p></p>
