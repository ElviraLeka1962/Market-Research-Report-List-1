<p><strong>The Vacuum Switching Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Gate Valve</li><li>Isolation Valve</li><li>Throttling Valve</li><li>Slit/Door Valve</li><li>Angle/Inline Valve</li><li>All Metal Valve</li><li>Pendulum Valve</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum switching valve market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. Butterfly valves manage flow with a rotating disc, while gate valves provide on/off control with a sliding gate. Isolation valves ensure system integrity by preventing flow, whereas throttling valves regulate flow rates. Slit or door valves offer precise control in high-vacuum environments. Angle and inline valves optimize space and direction of flow. All metal valves are durable for harsh conditions, and pendulum valves facilitate unique flow paths, while "others" include specialized or hybrid designs.</p></p>
