<p><strong>The Night-vision Goggles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The night-vision goggles market primarily consists of two types: image intensifier and thermal imaging systems. Image intensifier goggles amplify ambient light, allowing users to see in low-light conditions by enhancing visible light. In contrast, thermal imaging goggles detect heat signatures, enabling vision in total darkness by converting thermal energy into visible images. Both technologies serve various applications, including military, law enforcement, and outdoor activities, catering to specific operational needs and environments for improved visibility and situational awareness.</p></p>
