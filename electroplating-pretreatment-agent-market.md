<p><p>The Electroplating Pretreatment Agent Market is categorized into three types based on pH levels: acidic, neutral, and alkaline. Acidic pretreatment agents effectively remove rust and contaminants, enhancing metal surface quality for better adhesion. Neutral agents provide a balanced approach, minimizing corrosion while still preparing surfaces adequately. Alkaline pretreatment agents are effective in degreasing and cleaning, particularly for oily surfaces. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring optimal electroplating results by improving surface conditions prior to the plating process.</p></p>
