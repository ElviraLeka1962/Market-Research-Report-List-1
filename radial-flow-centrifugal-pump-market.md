<p><p>The radial flow centrifugal pump market is categorized into single-stage, two-stage, and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps are designed for simple applications requiring low to medium flow rates and pressure. Two-stage pumps offer increased pressure by utilizing two impellers, making them suitable for moderate flow and higher pressure needs. Multi-stage pumps contain several impellers, enabling them to achieve high pressures for applications like water supply and industrial processes, making them ideal for long-distance fluid transfer and high-lift scenarios.</p></p>
