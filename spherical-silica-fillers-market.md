<p><p>The spherical silica fillers market can be divided into three main types based on particle size: 0.01μm-10μm, 10μm-20μm, and above 20μm. Fillers in the 0.01μm-10μm range are used for applications requiring fine particle size, enhancing properties like viscosity and stability. The 10μm-20μm range offers a balance between strength and lightweight characteristics, suitable for various formulations. Fillers above 20μm are typically utilized for bulk applications where larger particles provide enhanced texture and improved mechanical performance in products.</p></p>
