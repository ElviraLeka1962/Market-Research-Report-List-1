<p><p>The bioplastics for packaging market encompasses various types that differ in composition and application. Bio PET is derived from renewable resources and is commonly used in bottles. Bio PA, or bio-based polyamide, offers better thermal properties. Bio PE, a bio-based polyethylene, is widely used in flexible packaging. Bio-PP, or bio-based polypropylene, provides durability. Bio-PS (polystyrene) is used for containers, while PLA (polylactic acid) is sought for its biodegradability. PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoates) are biopolymers from microbial processes. Starch blends combine renewable resources for versatile applications, while "Others" includes emerging bioplastic types.</p></p>
