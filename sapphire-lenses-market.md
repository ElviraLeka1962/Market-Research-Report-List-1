<p><p>The sapphire lenses market encompasses various types, including concave, convex, and planoconvex lenses. Concave lenses, which diverge light rays, are used in applications requiring image reduction and focus adjustment. Convex lenses converge light rays, making them ideal for magnification and focus in optical instruments. Planoconvex lenses, featuring one flat and one curved surface, provide uniform light distribution and are commonly used in laser applications. Together, these lens types cater to diverse industries, including aerospace, defense, and telecommunications.</p></p>
