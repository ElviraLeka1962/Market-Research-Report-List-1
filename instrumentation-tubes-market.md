<p><p>The instrumentation tubes market is primarily categorized into seamless tubes and welded tubes. Seamless tubes are manufactured through processes that produce a solid metal billet, resulting in high strength and resistance to pressure, making them ideal for critical applications. In contrast, welded tubes are formed by joining flat metal strips, offering advantages in cost-effectiveness and versatile lengths. Both types serve various industries, including oil and gas, automotive, and chemical, where precise measurements and reliability are essential.</p></p>
