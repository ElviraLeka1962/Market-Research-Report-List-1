<p><ul><li>Non-contact Type</li><li>Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fever thermometer market is divided into two main types: non-contact and contact thermometers. Non-contact thermometers use infrared technology to measure body temperature from a distance, making them quick and hygienic, ideal for screening large groups. Contact thermometers, including digital and mercury types, require placement against the skin or other surfaces to measure temperature accurately. While contact thermometers tend to be more accurate, non-contact models are increasingly popular for their convenience and safety in various settings, including clinics and homes.</p></p>
