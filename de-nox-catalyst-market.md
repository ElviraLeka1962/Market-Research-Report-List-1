<p><p>The De-NOx catalyst market primarily features two types: honeycomb and flat catalysts. Honeycomb catalysts have a cylindrical structure with numerous channels, offering high surface area for optimal nitrogen oxides (NOx) reduction. This design enhances gas flow and promotes efficient reactions, making them popular in automotive and industrial applications. In contrast, flat catalysts utilize a planar structure, suitable for specific applications and space-constrained environments. Both types play vital roles in meeting emissions standards and improving air quality.</p></p>
