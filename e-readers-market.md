<p><p>The e-reader market is primarily divided into two types: E-ink and TFT-LCD. In contrast, TFT-LCD e-readers employ backlit screens, offering vibrant colors and faster refresh rates suitable for multimedia content and interactive features. Each type caters to distinct user preferences, impacting their applications in reading, studying, and entertainment.</p></p>
