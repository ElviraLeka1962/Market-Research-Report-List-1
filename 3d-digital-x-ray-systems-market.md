<p><strong>The 3D Digital X-Ray Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Digital X-Ray Systems market encompasses two primary technologies: Computed Radiography (CR) and Digital Radiography (DR). CR Tech Digital X-Ray Systems utilize a cassette-based approach, capturing images on photostimulable phosphor plates that are later scanned and converted into digital images. On the other hand, DR Tech Digital X-Ray Systems capture images directly using digital detectors, providing rapid imaging and improved image quality. Both technologies are crucial for advanced diagnostic capabilities in medical imaging, enhancing patient care and operational efficiency.</p></p>
