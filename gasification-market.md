<p><p>Gasification is a thermochemical process that converts organic or fossil-based materials into syngas, a mixture primarily comprising carbon monoxide, hydrogen, and carbon dioxide. This process occurs at high temperatures and low oxygen levels, facilitating the transformation of solid fuels like coal, biomass, and waste into usable energy. The resulting syngas can be utilized for electricity generation, fuel production, and chemical manufacturing.</p><p>The Gasification Market is poised for significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for cleaner energy sources, rising energy prices, and the need for sustainable waste management solutions. Additionally, advancements in gasification technologies, such as integrated gasification combined cycle (IGCC) systems, are enhancing efficiency and reducing environmental impacts. </p><p>Latest trends indicate a focus on integrating gasification with renewable energy sources, such as biomass, to promote sustainability.
