<p><ul><li>In-Vivo</li><li>In-Vitro</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biophotonics market is primarily categorized into two types: in-vivo and in-vitro. In-vivo biophotonics involves imaging and analyzing biological processes within living organisms, providing real-time insights into cellular functions and disease progression. Conversely, in-vitro biophotonics encompasses analyses conducted outside of living organisms, often using isolated cells or tissues in controlled environments. Both types are essential for advancing medical research and diagnostics, enhancing understanding of biological mechanisms and improving therapeutic interventions.</p></p>
