<p><p>Ion trap mass analyzers are essential tools in mass spectrometry, categorized into three primary types. Quadrupole ion trap mass analyzers utilize electric fields to confine ions and enable mass selection, offering high sensitivity and resolution. Linear ion trap mass analyzers improve on this by extending the ion path, resulting in increased ion trapping efficiency and higher throughput. Other types might include cryogenic ion traps and Paul traps, each designed for specific applications and varying mass ranges, enhancing analytical capabilities across diverse fields.</p></p>
