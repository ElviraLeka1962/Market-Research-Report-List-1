<p><ul><li>Adapter Board</li><li>Demonstration Board</li><li>Evaluation Board</li><li>Add-On Board</li><li>Breakout Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sensor development boards market comprises various types catering to different needs. Adapter boards facilitate connection between sensors and microcontrollers, while demonstration boards showcase sensor capabilities in practical applications. Evaluation boards provide a platform for assessing sensor performance in real-world scenarios. Add-on boards enhance existing systems with additional sensors or features, whereas breakout boards simplify access to a sensor's pins and functions for prototyping. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing sensor technology and application development.</p></p>
