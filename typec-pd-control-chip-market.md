<p><strong>The TypeC PD Control Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UFP</li><li>DFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Type-C Power Delivery (PD) control chip market is categorized into two main types: Upstream Facing Port (UFP) and Downstream Facing Port (DFP). UFP devices, such as laptops and smartphones, receive power, while DFP devices, including chargers and power banks, supply power. Each type has distinct requirements for power negotiation and management, enabling efficient energy transfer. UFPs primarily focus on battery usage, whereas DFPs emphasize energy output capabilities, reflecting different roles within the ecosystem of connected devices.</p></p>
