<p><ul><li>Flat-Sheet Membrane</li><li>Pipe Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic inorganic membrane market includes two primary types: flat-sheet membranes and pipe membranes. Flat-sheet membranes feature a flat, planar design, ideal for various filtration applications due to their large surface area and ease of integration. Pipe membranes, on the other hand, have a tubular form, which enhances durability and flow rates, making them suitable for demanding processes like wastewater treatment and chemical separation. Both types leverage ceramic materials for superior thermal and chemical resistance, offering long-lasting filtration solutions.</p></p>
