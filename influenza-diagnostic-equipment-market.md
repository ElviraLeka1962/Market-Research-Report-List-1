<p><p>The influenza diagnostic equipment market comprises several key types. Rapid Influenza Diagnostic Tests (RIDTs) offer quick results for detecting viral antigens. Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction (RT-PCR) is a more sensitive molecular technique that identifies viral RNA, providing accurate diagnoses. Cell culture methods involve growing the virus in a laboratory setting for identification, which is often more time-consuming. Other diagnostic approaches may include serological tests or novel technologies, contributing to a diverse landscape of influenza detection solutions.</p></p>
