<p><p>The Strong Basic Anion Exchange Resin (SBA) market is categorized into two main types: Gel Strong Basic Anion Exchange Resin and Macroporous Strong Basic Anion Exchange Resin. Gel SBA resins feature a uniform gel structure, providing high efficiency in ion exchange with minimal mass transfer resistance, making them ideal for applications needing high purity. In contrast, Macroporous SBA resins have a larger pore structure that enhances capacity for bulk ion removal and ability to operate under varying conditions, offering versatile applications in water treatment and chemical processes.</p></p>
