<p><p>The biodegradable engine oil market comprises various types, each with unique properties. Vegetable oils, derived from plants, offer natural lubricating characteristics and are renewable. Polyalkylene glycols provide excellent thermal stability and are non-toxic. Synthetic esters, created through chemical synthesis, deliver high-performance lubrication and biodegradability. Together, these alternatives cater to eco-friendly automotive needs, reducing pollution and promoting sustainability in engine maintenance.</p></p>
