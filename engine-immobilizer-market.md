<p><p>The Engine Immobilizer Market includes **Mechanical** and **Electronic** types. **Mechanical immobilizers** use physical mechanisms like steering locks or key-based systems to prevent unauthorized vehicle access. They are simple and cost-effective but less secure. **Electronic immobilizers** rely on advanced technologies such as transponders, RFID, or biometric systems to disable the engine unless the correct key or code is used. They offer higher security and are widely adopted in modern vehicles, driving market growth due to increasing theft concerns and technological advancements.</p></p>
