<p><p>Wireless charging integrated circuits (ICs) are essential components enabling the transfer of power without physical connections. The market is divided into two main types: transmitter ICs and receiver ICs. Transmitter ICs manage the power output, ignition of the charging process, and communication protocols. In contrast, receiver ICs are responsible for receiving and converting the transmitted energy into usable power for devices. The growth of this market is driven by increasing demand for convenient charging solutions across consumer electronics and electric vehicles.</p></p>
