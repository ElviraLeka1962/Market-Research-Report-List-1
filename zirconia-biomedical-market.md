<p><p>The Zirconia biomedical market is primarily divided into two types: opaque zirconia and translucent zirconia. Opaque zirconia is used for applications requiring strength and durability, such as dental crowns and implants, where aesthetics are less crucial. Translucent zirconia, on the other hand, is favored for its natural appearance and light transmission, making it ideal for aesthetic dental restorations like veneers and bridges. Each type serves distinct needs within the dental and biomedical sectors, balancing function and visual appeal.</p></p>
