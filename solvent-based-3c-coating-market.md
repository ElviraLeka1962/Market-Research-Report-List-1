<p><strong>The Solvent-based 3C Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Coating</li><li>UV Curing Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solvent-based 3C coating market includes thermosetting and UV curing coatings. Thermosetting coatings, which undergo a chemical reaction when heated, offer excellent durability and resistance to heat and chemicals, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, UV curing coatings cure rapidly upon exposure to ultraviolet light, providing quick drying times and lower volatile organic compound emissions. Both types are widely used in automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, catering to different performance and environmental requirements within the coatings sector.</p></p>
