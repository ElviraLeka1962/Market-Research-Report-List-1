<p><p>The inhalation therapy nebulizer market comprises three main types: pneumatic nebulizers, ultrasonic nebulizers, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers, or jet nebulizers, use compressed air to convert liquid medication into aerosol. Ultrasonic nebulizers utilize high-frequency sound waves to produce mist, offering faster treatment. Mesh nebulizers employ a vibrating mesh to create aerosol, ensuring efficient and quiet operation. Each type serves different patient needs and preferences, driving innovation and customization in inhalation therapy solutions.</p></p>
