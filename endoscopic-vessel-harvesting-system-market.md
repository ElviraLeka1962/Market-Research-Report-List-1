<p><p>The Endoscopic Vessel Harvesting System market can be categorized into two main types: disposable and reusable systems. Disposable systems are designed for single-use, ensuring sterility, reducing infection risk, and eliminating the need for sterilization processes, thus enhancing operational efficiency. In contrast, reusable systems are engineered for multiple uses, providing cost-effectiveness over time but requiring rigorous cleaning and maintenance. Both types play a crucial role in minimally invasive surgeries, allowing for easier access and collection of veins for grafting procedures.</p></p>
