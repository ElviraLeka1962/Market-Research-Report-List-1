<p><p>The dog raw diet market consists of three primary types: frozen, freeze-dried, and dehydrated foods. Frozen raw diets are typically made from fresh proteins and require refrigeration, ensuring minimal nutrient loss. Freeze-dried options involve removing moisture while preserving nutrients, offering convenience and longer shelf life without refrigeration. Dehydrated diets also remove moisture but require rehydration before feeding, making them lightweight and easy to store. Each type caters to different preferences for storage, preparation, and nutritional needs.</p></p>
