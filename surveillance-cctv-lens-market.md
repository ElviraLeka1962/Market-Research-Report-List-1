<p><p>The surveillance (CCTV) lens market comprises three main types: fixed lenses, varifocal lenses, and zoom lenses. Fixed lenses offer a set focal length, providing a consistent field of view, ideal for specific monitoring tasks. Varifocal lenses allow users to adjust the focal length, offering flexibility in capturing varying scenes without changing the lens. Zoom lenses provide the ability to change the focal length dynamically and can focus on distant objects, making them suitable for diverse surveillance needs.</p></p>
