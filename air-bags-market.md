<p><p>The airbag market is categorized into several types based on their placement and function. Frontal airbags deploy from the steering wheel or dashboard to protect occupants during head-on collisions. Knee airbags mitigate injury to the lower body by inflating below the dashboard. Side airbags are designed to protect occupants in side-impact collisions, while curtain airbags deploy from the roof to shield passengers' heads from side impacts and rollovers. Each type enhances vehicle safety and plays a crucial role in occupant protection.</p></p>
