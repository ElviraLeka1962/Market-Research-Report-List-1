<p><p>Air curtains and unit heaters are essential in maintaining comfort in commercial and industrial spaces. The market is divided into recirculating and non-recirculating types. Recirculating air curtains recycle indoor air, preventing drafts while maintaining temperature, enhancing energy efficiency. In contrast, non-recirculating air curtains draw air from outside, providing fresh airflow but potentially losing indoor heat. Unit heaters work similarly, with recirculating types maximizing heat retention, while non-recirculating models focus on introducing warmed outdoor air, catering to specific environmental needs.</p></p>
