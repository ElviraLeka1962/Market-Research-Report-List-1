<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID electronic tags market is divided into two main types: passive RFID and active RFID. Passive RFID tags do not have a power source; they rely on energy from reader signals to operate, making them cost-effective for high-volume applications like inventory management. In contrast, active RFID tags are battery-powered, providing a longer range and real-time data transmission, ideal for tracking high-value assets or in environments where constant monitoring is critical. Each serves unique needs in various industries.</p></p>
