<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chromate-free epoxy primer market is segmented into single component and two component types. Single component primers are ready-to-use formulations that require no mixing, offering ease of application and quick drying times. In contrast, two component primers consist of a resin and a hardener that must be mixed prior to use, providing enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Both types are preferred for their environmentally friendly properties, eliminating chromates while delivering excellent adhesion and corrosion protection for various substrates.</p></p>
