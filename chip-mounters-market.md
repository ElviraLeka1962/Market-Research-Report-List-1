<p><p>Chip mounters are essential in the electronics manufacturing sector, primarily categorized into two market types: Surface Mount Technology (SMT) equipment and Through-Hole Technology (THT) equipment. SMT equipment focuses on mounting components directly onto the surface of printed circuit boards (PCBs), offering efficiency and compact designs for modern devices. In contrast, THT equipment involves inserting leads of components through holes in the PCB, providing robustness and reliability for specific applications. Both markets cater to distinct manufacturing needs and technologies.</p></p>
