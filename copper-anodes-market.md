<p><strong>The Copper Anodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxygen-free copper anodes</li><li>Phosphorous copper anodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Copper anodes are essential in electroplating and refining processes. Oxygen-free copper anodes are highly pure, with minimal oxygen content, making them ideal for applications requiring high conductivity and minimal oxidation, such as electronics and telecommunications. Phosphorous copper anodes contain a small amount of phosphorous, which improves grain structure and reduces surface oxidation during plating, making them suitable for decorative and industrial plating. Both types cater to specific needs, with oxygen-free anodes excelling in purity and phosphorous anodes offering enhanced plating performance.</p></p>
