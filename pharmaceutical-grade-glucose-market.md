<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Grade Glucose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>API Grade</li><li>Excipient Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade glucose market is divided into two main types: API grade and excipient grade. API grade glucose is used as an active pharmaceutical ingredient, essential for formulating medicines. It provides therapeutic effects, often serving as a source of energy or a vehicle for drug delivery. Excipient grade glucose, on the other hand, serves as an inactive ingredient in drug formulations, aiding in stabilization, solubility, and overall product efficacy. Both grades ensure high purity and safety for pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
