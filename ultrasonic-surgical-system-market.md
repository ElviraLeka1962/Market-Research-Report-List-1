<p><p>The Ultrasonic Surgical System market encompasses various technologies used for precise therapeutic applications. High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) Ablators use focused ultrasound waves to thermally ablate tissues, ideal for treating tumors. Magnetic Resonance-guided Focused Ultrasound (MRGFUS) Ablators combine ultrasound with MRI for real-time imaging and guidance during procedures. Ultrasonic Surgical Ablation Systems provide broader ultrasonic capabilities for surgical applications, while Shock Wave Therapy Systems utilize acoustic waves to promote healing and reduce pain. Each system offers unique advantages for minimally invasive treatments.</p></p>
