<p><p>Dental bridges and crowns are restorative dental procedures aimed at replacing missing teeth and restoring the function and appearance of the mouth. A dental crown is a cap placed over a tooth to restore its shape, size, strength, and improve its appearance. It can also be used to cover dental implants. A dental bridge, on the other hand, consists of one or more artificial teeth anchored to adjacent natural teeth, effectively filling the gap left by missing teeth.</p><p>The Dental Bridge and Crown Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by several factors, including an increasing aging population susceptible to dental issues, growing awareness about oral health, and advancements in dental technology. Recent trends indicate a shift towards materials that offer better durability and aesthetics, such as zirconia and porcelain.
