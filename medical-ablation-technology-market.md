<p><p>Medical ablation technology encompasses various techniques aimed at destroying undesired tissue, primarily used for cancer treatment. Thermal ablation employs heat to target and eliminate cells. Radiofrequency ablation uses radio waves to generate heat for tissue destruction. Microwave ablation employs microwave energy for similar purposes, offering quick procedures. Laser ablation utilizes focused light to vaporize tissue, allowing precise targeting. Cryoablation entails freezing tissue to induce cell death. Each type presents unique advantages, catering to specific medical needs and conditions.</p></p>
