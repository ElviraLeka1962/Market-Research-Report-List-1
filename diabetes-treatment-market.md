<p><strong>The Diabetes Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Insulin</li><li>Oral Hypoglycaemic Drugs</li><li>Non-Insulin Injectable Drugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diabetes treatment market comprises three main types: insulin, oral hypoglycemic drugs, and non-insulin injectable drugs. Insulin is crucial for Type 1 diabetes management and some Type 2 cases, aiding blood sugar control directly. Oral hypoglycemic agents are commonly prescribed for Type 2 diabetes, working by enhancing insulin sensitivity or stimulating insulin production. Non-insulin injectables, such as GLP-1 receptor agonists, offer alternative treatment options, promoting weight loss and improving glycemic control without the need for insulin.</p></p>
