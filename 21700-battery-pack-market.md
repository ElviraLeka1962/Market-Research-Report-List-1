<p><p>The 21700 battery pack market features two primary types: series and parallel battery packs. Series battery packs connect multiple cells in a chain, increasing voltage while maintaining the same capacity, making them ideal for high-voltage applications. In contrast, parallel battery packs connect cells side by side, enhancing overall capacity without altering voltage, which is beneficial for applications requiring sustained power. Both configurations cater to various industries, including electric vehicles, energy storage systems, and portable electronics, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
