<p><p>The microfluidic pumps market includes various types of pumps, each suited for specific applications. Syringe pumps provide precise volume control by forcing fluid through a syringe, making them ideal for accurate drug delivery and research. Peristaltic pumps use a rotating mechanism to compress tubing, allowing for sterile fluid transfer without contamination, commonly used in biochemical applications. Pressure pumps generate fluid movement through pressure differentials, offering versatility in high-flow scenarios, often utilized in industrial processes and microfabrication.</p></p>
