<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Distance Meter</li><li>Laser Distance Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The distance meter market primarily includes two types: ultrasonic and laser distance meters. Ultrasonic distance meters use sound waves to measure distance, making them suitable for indoor applications where obstacles may be present. They are typically cost-effective and easy to use. In contrast, laser distance meters utilize laser beams for precise measurements, delivering high accuracy over longer distances. These are preferred in professional settings such as construction and surveying where precision is crucial. Both technologies cater to varying user needs.</p></p>
