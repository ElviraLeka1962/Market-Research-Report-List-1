<p><p>The Digital Fabrication Inkjet Inks market comprises two primary types based on the composition of inks: dye-based and pigment-based inks. Dye-based inks offer vibrant colors and excellent smoothness, making them ideal for photographic applications but can be less resistant to UV light and water. In contrast, pigment-based inks provide greater durability, fade resistance, and longevity, making them suitable for outdoor applications and archival prints. Both types cater to diverse printing needs across various industries, influencing manufacturing processes and product outcomes.</p></p>
