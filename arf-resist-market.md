<p><ul><li>ArF Dry Resist</li><li>ArF Immersion Resist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ArF resist market is primarily categorized into two types: ArF dry resist and ArF immersion resist. ArF dry resist is utilized in standard photolithography processes, featuring lower viscosity and easier application, making it suitable for high-throughput manufacturing. In contrast, ArF immersion resist is designed for advanced lithography techniques, enhancing resolution and pattern fidelity by using a liquid medium to improve light refraction. Both types are vital for semiconductor fabrication, catering to varying resolution and performance requirements.</p></p>
