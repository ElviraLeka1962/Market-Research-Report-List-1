<p><p>The 6-axis IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit) market comprises three main types: digital output, analog output, and a combination of both. Digital output sensors provide processed data in a standardized format, ideal for integration with microcontrollers and digital systems. Analog output sensors deliver continuous voltage signals that reflect acceleration and rotation, suitable for systems requiring real-time data without processing latency. The hybrid option combines both outputs, offering flexibility for various applications, ensuring compatibility with diverse technological environments.</p></p>
