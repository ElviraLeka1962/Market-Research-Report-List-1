<p><p>Military surveillance drones are categorized into three main types: **Fixed Wing Drones**, which resemble airplanes, offering long-range and high-speed capabilities ideal for large-area monitoring. **Rotary Blade Drones**, like quadcopters, provide vertical takeoff, hover, and precise maneuverability, suitable for close-range reconnaissance. **Hybrid Drones** combine features of both, enabling vertical takeoff and long-endurance flight, making them versatile for varied missions. Each type caters to specific operational needs, balancing range, endurance, and flexibility in military surveillance applications.</p></p>
