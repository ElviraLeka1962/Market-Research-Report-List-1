<p><strong>The Electric Sliding Door Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top-mounted Type</li><li>Ground Rail Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric sliding door market features two primary types: top-mounted and ground rail. The top-mounted type uses an overhead track for smooth operation, ideal for spaces with limited floor clearance. It's often favored for residential applications due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of installation. In contrast, the ground rail type operates along a track embedded in the floor, providing enhanced stability and durability, making it well-suited for commercial spaces with high foot traffic. Both types offer automated convenience and security.</p></p>
